About

My name is Daniel Li. I'm a hobbyist programmer, artist, and musician who likes aesthetics and open source. I'm currently studying computer science at the University of Florida.

InfiniteLimits is my brand name. To me, it represents a juxtaposition of two competing ideas: first, on the existence of physical, social, and psychological boundaries on all aspects of life — “limits,” if you will. One should always accept that difficulties will be waiting at every turn. But second, on the interminable possibility of growth and humanity's unfailing ambition to extend these boundaries — our “infinite” capacity to learn, grow, and fight for positive change, to overcome the barriers that hold us back.

The fifth InfiniteLimits logo highlights hidden unity: the slanted rectangle created by the borders of the geometry represents the order we strive to find in the world and the relationships we seek through it.
