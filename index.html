DANIEL LI

I'm a hobbyist programmer, artist, and musician who likes aesthetics and open source. I'm currently studying computer science at the University of Florida.

Why InfiniteLimits? To me, this phrase is a juxtaposition of two competing ideas. The first is the existence of physical, social, and psychological boundaries on all aspects of life — “limits,” if you will. One can only accept that difficulties will be waiting at every turn, that there will always remain something tantalizingly out of reach. But the second is the interminable possibility of growth and humanity's unfailing ambition to extend these boundaries — our “infinite” capacity to learn, grow, and fight for positive change, to overcome the barriers that hold us back.
